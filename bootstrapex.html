Every year millions of animals are left homeless on the streets or in animal shelters. Many people are aware of this issue, still they turn to puppy stores to buy their dog, helping to support puppy mills. You can make a huge difference in an animal's life by adopting. This website helps to counter adoption stigmas and explains the dangers of puppy mills. Click the dropdown menu to get started.
